Terms

Please read carefully as you need to agree to the terms below to submit the work arrangement agreement.

Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) requirements

I have reviewed and confirmed (by checking the appropriate boxes) that all of the requirements below have been met or steps have been taken to ensure they will be.

Mandatory Training and Awareness

  • Security Awareness (A230)
  • Workplace Hazardous Materials Information System (WHMIS)
  • Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Training for Managers and Supervisors (as per the Canada Labour Code Part II)(if applicable)
  • W101 Harassment and Violence Prevention for Employees
  • W102 Harassment and Violence Prevention for Managers, and OHS Committees/Representatives

Working Alone

You must ensure that if you are working alone, that you have reviewed the TC Working Alone Directive and have arranged with your supervisor to develop and abide by a working alone plan (if applicable).

Additional Considerations

If you have any questions regarding ergonomic guidelines or the proper set-up of your virtual workstation, you should discuss this with your supervisor. You should also review the following documents:

  • Preventing Musculoskeletal Injury E-tool (HRSDC)
  • The Canadian Centre for Occupational Health and Safety (CCOHS) offers workplace specific information on ergonomics.

Emergency Contact Management

I confirm that my emergency contact information in the Personal Information section of my myTC account is up to date. I understand that it is my responsibility to maintain and promptly update this information as required.

Information Technology, Information Management and Security of Information

I agree to use equipment and electronic networks belonging to the employer only for the purposes of carrying out the employee’s work unless otherwise authorized by the supervisor and/or described in the TBS Guideline on Acceptable Network and Device Use.

I agree to take all reasonable precautions to protect the equipment used and to take responsibility for the safeguarding of information (physical or electronic) at the telework location in accordance with the TC Protected and Classified Information Standard.

I agree to manage information of business value according to the requirements under the Access to Information Act and the Privacy Act.

I agree to store information of business value with a security level up to and including Protected B in Transport Canada's approved databases or its official document repository RDIMS according to the TC Information Management Directive.

I will ensure that a secure internet connection is used at my telework location at my telework location, and that to reduce risk of a cybersecurity breach, unsecure publicly available internet connections will not be used.

I agree to review the guidance provided by the Canadian Centre for Cyber Security ITSAP.00.087 Mobile Devices and Business travelers.

Employee Responsibilities for telework

I recognize that the Crown will not be held liable for an employee’s negligent misuse of government equipment that results in damages to the employee’s personal or real property during the performance of official duties or while using government equipment at the employee’s telework location.

I have completed or will complete the TC-required OHS training. I commit to maintaining a safe personal telework workspace. I recognize that my employer is responsible for my telework work tasks only, not for my entire home. I agree to report to my supervisor any accident, injury, illness, discomfort, ergonomic issues and hazards as soon as possible. Any accident, injury or illness arising from teleworking will only be considered work-related if arising from the performance of my work-related duties.

Transport Canada reserves the right to inspect the telework location I have identified. I recognize that in the event of a workplace accident, I may be required to allow access to investigators to perform their duties and recommend the appropriate corrective action and non-cooperation may impact my WAA.

Note : If you have any questions regarding the above conditions, contact your manager or Regional OHS Advisor, where applicable.

General conditions

I agree to abide by and respect the TC Guidelines for Telework Arrangements and more specifically

  • Assuming the costs of maintaining my personal telework workspace, such as insurance, heat, hydro, basic internet and telephone, etc., unless otherwise covered under a Government of Canada policy instrument.
  • Safeguarding Transport Canada’s equipment and assuming responsibility for the safe transportation of government equipment, and any associated costs incurred between worksites
  • Ensuring the protection of information by respecting the applicable security requirements
  • Understanding that any access to TC's network outside of Canada is prohibited, either through TC-issued or personal devices unless approved by the Deputy Minister. A Departmental Security briefing is required to ensure that all necessary approvals are obtained prior to commencement.
  • Conforming with information technology and information management directives, standards and procedures

Please note: The above terms will be sent to you in an email once your work arrangement has been approved by the delegated manager.
